Albatross Speech Codec (SEMI-TTS)

Experimental phoneme-based speech synthesis & speech codec system
Rule-based synthetic speech system (non-neural TTS / codec hybrid)
Training and Learning Purposes: you could transform your full name to robotic music
What is this?
A research-grade speech codec that converts text into robotic phoneme-based audio, and decodes audio back into text using frequency analysis.

The system is informed by signal processing research and explores extensions into nonlinear and chaotic signal dynamics, with future capability to mimic chaotic acoustic phenomena for advanced encoding, obfuscation, and synthetic speech generation.
